Adding a sitemap to your site:
    A sitemap is an XML file that tells search engines the pages of your website, their relevance, and how frequently they are updated.

The Django sitemap framework depends on 
 django.contrib.sites, 
 which allows you to associate objects to particular websites that are running with your project.
